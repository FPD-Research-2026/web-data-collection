# Informed Consent

**Study Title:** Your Information

Your Information

I invite you to participate in a research study. This form will help you
decide if you want to participate in data collection for this research. The
experimental study will take approximately 60 minutes of your time.
Participation in this research is voluntary.

## 1. What is the purpose of this study?

As social media and digital personas play an increasingly large role in our
day-to-day lives, digital identity verification is increasingly important.
This study seeks to create a machine-learning model that analyzes users'
behavioral footprints. For example, while users interact with social media
platforms, they type, swipe, scroll, etc. The machine learning model will
"learn" these behavioral patterns to measure the similarity among social
media profiles.

## 2. What will be done if you take part in this research study?

The participants will be briefed about the project and explained that
participation in the study is voluntary. Once they decide to participate,
they will sign the consent form. Then, we would provide login credentials
for dummy social media profiles for different social media platforms
(Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter). The participants would log in to the
social media platforms and create posts by typing (no copy-paste is allowed
as we collect typing patterns). The topic of the social media posts shall
revolve around but not limited to politics, economics, and current affairs.
In particular, the participants shall be given a series of videos to watch.
To complete a session, the participant will watch all the videos and, after
watching each video, will post their thoughts/emotions on Facebook,
Instagram, and Twitter. They will repeat that process for the second session
as well.

Throughout the exercises, participants' text and keystroke timing patterns
(timings of key press and release) will be recorded and saved.

## 3. What are the possible discomforts and risks?

Participants may experience minor discomfort when typing on a computer that
is not their own. However, beyond this, no significant risks or discomforts
are anticipated in this study.

## 4. What are the possible benefits to you or others?

You will learn about such an advanced study, be notified of the results, and
may receive extra credit for participating in it.

## 5. Confidentiality

During the data collection phase, the following information will be
collected: handedness (left or right), age, gender, and education level. The
investigator(s) will safely keep all the data and PII in a
password-protected drive. This demographic data is being collected to
analyze the bias of the detection methods across people of different age
groups, genders, and educational backgrounds. The anonymized data shall be
published to conduct future research in this domain.

## 6. If you choose to participate in this study, will it cost you anything?

There is no cost for participating in this study. You can withdraw from the
study at any point in time.
